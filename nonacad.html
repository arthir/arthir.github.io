Interests
I've been learning Bharatanatyam since I was 5 and was a member of Kalaa in my time at Princeton. I have also danced with Navatman as a member of their dance troupe. I have also learned bits of other dance styles such as salsa and swing.

I learnt Carnatic music for 3-4 years during my years in India which sparked the interest in my undergraduate thesis. I doubt I could actually identify any raagas anymore.
